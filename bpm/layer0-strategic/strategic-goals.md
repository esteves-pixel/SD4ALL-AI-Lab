# SD4ALL — Strategic Layer (Layer 0)

## Mission

Empower good people to become more capable — through expert self-defense training, community, and science-driven methodology.

## Vision

By 2028, SD4ALL is the leading capability-driven self-defense academy in the region — 300+ active students, a pipeline of trained instructors, and AI-powered operations that let us focus on what matters: our people.

## Core Values

- **Capability over aggression** — We build calm, capable people, not fighters
- **Science-driven** — Biomechanics, ecological learning, research-backed methods
- **Community first** — Good people becoming more capable together
- **Systems thinking** — Every process is documented, measured, and improved
- **Human + AI** — Agents support execution, humans own the mission

## Strategic Goals (OKRs)

### Goal 1: Grow student base to 200 by Dec 2026
- KR1: Increase lead → trial conversion rate to 40%
- KR2: Achieve consultation show-up rate of 75%
- KR3: Reach 6-week retention rate of 50%

### Goal 2: Build operational independence from founder
- KR1: Reduce Gonçalo's teaching load to <50% of classes
- KR2: Graduate 2 new instructors through Trainer of Trainers program
- KR3: Automate 5+ business processes with AI agents

### Goal 3: Become data-driven
- KR1: All 7 macro processes documented with SIPOC + KPIs
- KR2: Weekly automated KPI reports (revenue, retention, attendance)
- KR3: Predictive models for churn and revenue forecasting
