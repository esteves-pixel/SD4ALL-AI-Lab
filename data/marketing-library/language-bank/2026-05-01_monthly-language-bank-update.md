## 3. RAW EMOTIONAL LANGUAGE FROM REDDIT & ONLINE COMMUNITIES
*(Aggregated from Reddit summaries, Quora discussions, and community content indexed May 2026. Exact phrases where confirmed; paraphrased where summarized.)*

### Fear & Daily Safety Math
- *"I never walk within arm's reach of a doorway, a parked car, or an alleyway at night"*
- *"I wear headphones with no music — so I can ignore men but still hear if someone's behind me"*
- *"Parking lots give me so much anxiety when I'm alone"*
- *"I calculate the safest route every time I leave somewhere"*
- *"I stay on the phone with someone until I'm inside"*
- Women describe doing **constant threat math** that men report finding eye-opening — this language is viral when surfaced

### Freeze Response — Shame & Self-Blame
- *"I always assumed I'd fight back — I had no idea I could just freeze"*
- *"Sometimes the body just shuts down, full stop"*
- *"The majority of people under attack freeze — whether it's a mugging, an assault, or an aggressive person"*
- *"Freezing is not a personal failing"*
- *"Freezing does not mean you wanted it. It does not mean you are weak."*
- *"It took a long time to forgive myself for what my body did to try to protect me"*
- **STAT:** 70% of women experienced some degree of tonic immobility during sexual assault (peer-reviewed, 2025). 48% experienced extreme tonic immobility.
- Shame around freezing is the #1 unspoken barrier. Women believe they failed. Training reframes this.

### Objections to Starting a Class
- *"It's really intimidating to sign up for a self-defense class"*
- *"I don't know where to start"*
- *"I'm not athletic enough"*
- *"I'm not in shape — I'd be embarrassed"*
- *"I assumed I had to already know something before going"*
- *"I didn't think it was for someone like me"*

### What Finally Made Women Start
- A scary incident (personal or to someone they know)
- A friend or coworker who was already going ("buddy-pull" is huge)
- Reading about freeze response and feeling seen / not alone
- Realizing their current tools (keys between fingers, pepper spray) might not work when they freeze
- Morris County: Florham Park PD recurring seminar had to **add dates** due to demand — women are showing up when a trusted local authority invites them

### Transformation Language (Post-Training)
- *"When I was 28, I took my first self-defense class and learned that I did have options — that I did have power"*
- Women describe changed body language, walking differently, feeling less like a target
- *"I defend myself isn't solely about physical prowess — it encompasses mental strength"*
